Section 03 — Critical Incidents · EQUATE 2026
⚠️ High-Severity Equipment Incidents
Equipment 🔴 LTI 📅 1 Jan 2026 · EQUATE
Tailing Lug Failure – Trial Lift
During a trial lift of the bypass stack (~55 tonnes), the tailing lug detached unexpectedly, causing the load to drop approximately 500 mm to the ground. Minor dent resulted on the bottom shell plate.
Root Causes
• Change not formally assessed or controlled
• Critical hazards not identified prior to execution
• Work planning failed to account for actual conditions
💡 Lesson: All lifting plans must include a dynamic risk assessment at time of execution, not only at planning stage.
Equipment 🔴 LTI 📅 12 Feb 2026 · EQUATE
Pipeline Lateral Shift – Confined Space
During work resumption, a fabricator sustained a hip fracture after being struck by a 24-inch pipeline that shifted laterally while he was working in a confined space of only 1.1–1.4 m between two pipelines.
Root Causes
• AWD removed without proper hazard assessment
• Confined space risk not re-evaluated on resumption
• No dynamic restraint system in place
💡 Lesson: Work resumption must trigger a full restart assessment — treat it as a new permit, not a continuation.
